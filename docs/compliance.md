# Railora Compliance Caveat

Railora in this repository is a prototype. It is not licensed, regulated, or production-ready.

The app must not be used to:

- Hold or transmit real customer funds.
- Represent completed UAE PASS, DUL, KYC, KYB, sanctions, PEP, VAT, TRN, or e-invoicing checks.
- Issue legally binding escrow instructions.
- Provide credit decisions or regulated scoring.

Before production use, Railora would need legal review, licensing analysis, AML/KYC controls, data protection review, provider contracts, audited ledger operations, incident response, customer support operations, and production-grade security monitoring.
